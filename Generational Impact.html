OUR COMPANY

Our Aim

  • Generational Impact seeks to eliminate financial barriers for youths to join by offering a member Scholarship Program.

  • We are committed to giving a voice to and including diverse perspectives for all youths.

  • We will work to identify potential scholarship recipients from our nonprofit grantees and from across the County to encourage additional diverse perspectives and contributions to our membership.

  • In addition, Generation Impact members can specifically nominate someone for a scholarship whom they feel would bring insight and value to our program.

  • Together, with different backgrounds & perspectives, we can make a greater impact in our community.
